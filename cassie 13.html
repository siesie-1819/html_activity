Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 2 tbsp sweetened bananas
  • 2 tbsp sweetened kamote
  • 2 tbsp mongo beans (sweetened)
  • 2 tbsp nata de coco
  • 2 tbsp kaong
  • 2 tbsp gulaman
  • 2 tbsp mais (sweet corn)
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream
  • 1 slice leche flan
  • Evaporated milk for drizzling

Steps

  1. Prepare the glass: Use a tall glass or bowl. Add all sweetened ingredients layer by layer at the bottom.
  2. Add crushed ice: Fill the glass with crushed ice on top of the mix-ins.
  3. Drizzle milk: Pour evaporated milk evenly over the ice.
  4. Add toppings: Place a scoop of ube ice cream and a slice of leche flan on top.
  5. Serve: Enjoy as is or mix everything together before eating — kaya nga "halo-halo"!

Tip: Chill all ingredients beforehand para mas malamig at mas refreshing.

Halo-Halo — colorful, creamy, and the perfect pang-init!
