About Me

Getting to know the person behind this blog

Hi! My name is Cassie, and I am a proud fan of SB19. I created this blog to share my personal journey, thoughts, and experiences as someone who slowly discovered and fell in love with their music.

I enjoy listening to music, watching performances, and discovering the stories behind every song. SB19 inspired me not only through their talent but also through their perseverance, authenticity, and passion for their craft.

This blog serves as a space where I can express myself, document my fandom journey, and connect with other people who appreciate music, creativity, and meaningful stories. Being an A’TIN is something I am truly proud of.
