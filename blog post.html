My SB19 Journey

How curiosity slowly turned into genuine fandom

My First Encounter with SB19

March 2021

The first time I encountered SB19 was during the release of their song “MAPA”. At that time, I wasn’t really a fan yet. I heard the song, appreciated the emotions, but I didn’t fully understand the group behind it.

MAPA felt sincere and touching, but I was just a listener back then. I didn’t follow their updates, watch interviews, or search for their content. It was simply a moment of awareness, not yet connection.

How I Became a Fan

November 2023

Everything changed when I randomly saw a clip from ShowBreak Episode 5 the Whisper Challenge on TikTok. It was funny, natural, and very different from what I expected from a music group.

I laughed so hard that I ended up searching “SB19 funny moments” out of curiosity. One video led to another, and before I realized it, I was already watching interviews, performances, and behind-the-scenes clips. That was the moment I knew I had become a fan.

From Stell to Pablo: Finding My Bias

Present

At first, my bias was Stell. His humor, confidence, and strong stage presence really stood out to me. He was entertaining and easy to like, especially in variety shows and challenges.

However, as I learned more about the group, my bias slowly shifted to Pablo. His songwriting, leadership, and depth as an artist impressed me deeply. I admired how he expresses emotions through music and how he leads the group with sincerity and purpose.

In the end, SB19 is a group where every member shines in their own way. My journey as a fan continues, and this blog is where I choose to share that experience.
