Itlog with Pancit Canton

Ingredients

  • 1 pack pancit canton (any flavor)
  • 1–2 pcs egg
  • 1½ cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water: In a small pot, heat water until it begins to boil.
  2. Add the noodles: Place the pancit canton in the boiling water.
  3. Cook the egg: You can choose:
    • Poached style: Crack the egg directly into the pot and let it cook beside the noodles.
    • Fried egg: In a separate pan, fry the egg sunny-side-up or scrambled.
    • Boiled egg: Boil the egg for 8–10 minutes, peel, and slice.
  4. Add seasonings: Once the noodles soften, add the canton flavoring and mix well.
  5. Combine: Place the cooked noodles in a bowl and top with your egg of choice.
  6. Serve: Add salt, pepper, or a bit of oil for extra flavor.

Tip: For a tastier pancit canton, add spring onions or a few drops of calamansi.
