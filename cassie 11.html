Inihaw na Bangus

Ingredients

  • 1 whole bangus (milkfish), cleaned and gutted
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (optional)
  • Banana leaves or foil for wrapping

Steps

  1. Prepare the stuffing: Mix tomatoes, onions, garlic, and ginger. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Stuff the bangus: Fill the inside of the bangus with the mixture. Drizzle soy sauce if desired.
  3. Wrap the fish: Wrap the stuffed bangus in banana leaves or aluminum foil to keep it moist while grilling.
  4. Grill: Place the wrapped bangus on a preheated grill. Cook for 15–20 minutes on each side or until fish is fully cooked.
  5. Unwrap and serve: Remove the leaves/foil and serve hot with rice and dipping sauce (toyomansi).

Tip: Use banana leaves for extra aroma, and don’t overcook to keep the bangus moist and soft.

Inihaw na Bangus — smoky, juicy, and full of flavor.
