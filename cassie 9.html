Beef Steak (Bistek in Tagalog)

Ingredients

  • 500g beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 pcs lemon or 4 pcs calamansi
  • 1 large onion, sliced into rings
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup water (optional, for more sauce)

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef: Combine soy sauce, calamansi/lemon juice, garlic, and pepper. Add beef slices and marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. Sear the beef: Heat oil in a pan. Sear the beef slices until lightly browned. Set aside.
  3. Cook the onions: In the same pan, sauté onion rings until soft but not mushy. Remove some for topping later.
  4. Simmer: Return the beef to the pan. Pour the marinade and add water if you want more sauce. Simmer until beef becomes tender.
  5. Season: Taste and adjust—add more soy sauce or calamansi if needed.
  6. Serve: Top with the sautéed onion rings and serve hot with rice.

Tip: Make sure the beef slices are thin so they cook tender and evenly.

Bistek Tagalog — savory, tangy, and perfect with rice.
