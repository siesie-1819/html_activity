The official SB19 logo is an emblem combining a diamond and a windmill to symbolize their bond with fans (the diamond) and their reliance on fan support (the windmill), representing their journey, struggles, and aspirations to become enduring gems in the music industry. The group's name itself combines "SB" for Sound Break, their mission to make an impact, and "19" for the sum of the Philippines (+63) and South Korea (+82) country codes (6+3+8+2=19), signifying the collaboration between the two nations that led to their debut.
